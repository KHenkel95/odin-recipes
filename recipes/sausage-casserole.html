Sausage Casserole

Description

There is no more simple a Casserole than this. With only 4 ingredients you can sit and eat in no time.

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans refrigerated crescent rolls

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease the bottom of a 9x11-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Stir in cream cheese until combined.
  4. Roll crescent roll dough from 1 can into the bottom of the prepared baking dish without separating. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Spread sausage mixture over baked crescent rolls; top with crescent roll dough from remaining can.
  6. Continue baking until the top layer is golden brown, about 8 minutes.
